Workbrew builds the features companies need on top of Homebrew, the most popular package manager on macOS and a rapidly growing standard on Linux. We work at the intersection of open source and the enterprise, helping teams adopt Homebrew with confidence through commercial support, MDM integration, and emerging enterprise capabilities.

The role

We’re looking for a Designer to join our Go-to-market team on a contract basis. This role is open to designers at any stage of their career. You’ll help shape how Workbrew shows up in the world, turning ideas into clear, expressive design across brand and marketing touchpoints.

This is a hands-on, creative role for someone who is excited by open source, enjoys inventing new concepts, and wants space to grow their craft alongside a fast-moving company.
