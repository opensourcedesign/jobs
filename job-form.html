Submit a job to Open Source Design

Let the community know you are looking for design contributions. If your free / open source project needs some design work, use this form to submit a job to Open Source Design.

We have a code of conduct. Consider reading it before submitting your job.

About you

The name of your company, organisation or project.

A link to the organisation you entered above.

About the job

This will be the main heading in your job's page. Make it specific, but not too long. You could include a role and the name of your project.

The type of remuneration on offer.

Give some information about the payment offered. Try to be specific. A fixed amount or a range are good. Avoid things like "competitive": they are not very informative.

Include a brief introduction to your project, and explain the design work needed.

Markdown is okay

If appropriate, list the things you expect the designer to deliver. For example: a logo and a set of icons.
